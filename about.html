About

20 years of growing brands through acquisition.

I'm Matt Burgess. I've spent two decades helping businesses find, convert, and keep customers — across every major digital channel.

The short version

I'm a performance marketer who's spent 20 years in the trenches of customer acquisition. I've managed multi-million dollar budgets, built and led marketing teams, and worked across industries from travel and tourism to financial services and comparison.

I started my career in SEO — at Wotif Group, managing organic traffic across some of APAC's largest travel brands. From there I moved into broader digital marketing roles, eventually leading teams that spanned SEO, PPC, content, email, social, and display.

Today, I'm Head of Marketing at The Motley Fool Australia, where I oversee new member acquisition. On the side, I consult through Conversation Media for businesses that want senior-level marketing expertise without the overhead of a full agency.
