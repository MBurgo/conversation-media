Most Google Ads accounts are leaving money on the table

The most common problems I see: fragmented campaign structures spreading budget too thin, Performance Max cannibalising branded search, keyword overlap causing you to bid against yourself, and conversion tracking that's either broken or measuring the wrong things.

These aren't exotic problems. They're structural issues that accumulate quietly, and they're costing you money every day they go unfixed.

What I bring to the table

I'm Google Ads certified and have managed significant budgets across Search, Shopping, Display, YouTube, and Performance Max. My approach is audit-first — I don't make changes until I understand exactly what's happening in your account and why.

  • Comprehensive account audit — structure, keywords, match types, bidding, ad copy, extensions, and tracking
  • Campaign restructuring to eliminate waste and consolidate budget where it performs
  • Search campaign management — keyword strategy, negative keyword hygiene, ad copy testing
  • Performance Max setup and optimisation — asset groups, audience signals, and feed management
  • Conversion tracking audit and implementation — making sure you're measuring what matters
  • Monthly reporting with clear insight into what's working and what's next
