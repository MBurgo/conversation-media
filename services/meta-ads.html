Meta Ads Management

Campaigns built to convert, not just look pretty.

Meta is a creative-first platform now. I build and manage campaigns that pair smart audience strategy with direct response creative.

The problem with most Meta Ads accounts

Most businesses are either boosting posts and hoping for the best, or paying an agency that's spread across too many clients to give their account the attention it needs. The result is the same: wasted spend, weak creative, and no clear path from click to customer.

Meta's advertising platform has changed dramatically in the last few years. Broad targeting works. Advantage+ is powerful — when set up correctly. But creative is now the primary lever. The algorithm will find your audience if you give it ads worth showing.

What I do differently

I don't just manage your campaigns. I write the ads. My background in direct response copywriting means every piece of creative is built to drive action — not just engagement. There's a meaningful difference between an ad that gets likes and an ad that gets customers.

  • Full account audit and restructuring
  • Campaign architecture — Advantage+, manual setups, or hybrid approaches based on what your business actually needs
  • Creative strategy and ad copywriting — hooks, angles, and formats designed to convert
  • Audience strategy — prospecting, retargeting, and lookalike development
  • Landing page review and conversion rate recommendations
  • Full-funnel measurement, attribution, and reporting

Who this is for

This works best for businesses spending $3,000+ per month on Meta Ads who want a senior strategist managing their account — not a junior. If you're getting started and need help building from scratch, I can do that too.

How it works

Every engagement starts with an audit. I'll review your current account, creative, tracking, and funnel — then present what I'd change and why. If we're a good fit, we move forward. If not, you walk away with a clear picture of where the problems are.
